Görli Testnet is a community-built proof-of-authority cross-client testnet. The first one of its kind. So it’s only natural that the first testnet conference would be centred around it, where it was born, in Berlin.
What will it involve? There will be hands-on sessions, presentations, and a discussion on how to support open-source contributors.
The project has great potential to alleviate the current testnets that exist in the space, and give dApp developers from both the ETH and ETC ecosystem, the possibility to have a more robust testnet, one able to adapt to their needs, to work with, and we want to make that happen.
Tech and non-tech people are welcome to join the one-day conference. Especially if you consider yourself an open-source advocate. No previous experience needed, as we’ll have an onboarding session at the start of the day.
In addition, as Görli Testnet was born at ETHBerlin, we want to throw a reunion for the hackathon. If you were a hacker, a sponsor, or a fan, please get in touch for our show & tell session.
About your Kickback contribution: We are setting up a 45 EUR deposit to come to the event. As in Kickback’s usual fashion, you can either choose to claim it back once you arrive, or donate to the project - this is entirely up to you and no fingers will be pointed. All proceeds will go to funding Görli Testnet.
The event includes food and drinks - this is hosted & funded by the Department of Decentralization, the artists formerly known as ETHBerlin. We look forward to everyone joining, and if you have ideas, well, we got a form and eager to hear from you!

About Görli Testnet

.---------. |.-------.| ||>run# || || || |"-------'| .-^---------^-. |Görli Testnet| "-------------'
Görli Testnet is the first proof-of-authority cross-client testnet, synching Parity Ethereum, Geth, Nethermind, Pantheon, and EthereumJS. This testnet is a community-based project, completely open-source, naturally. It was born in September 2018 during ETHBerlin and has been growing in contributors ever since. More info here.
There are several bounties up for people that want to contribute. Please read the bounty announcement for the details. Last but not least, we are building our validator network! So if you are interested in spinning up your own node, road the deployment guide.

If you would like to contribute in any shape or form, we are always looking for new people to join the project. If you are technical our discussions are on Gitter. Join us! Check the open issues on Github.
There is room for non-technical people as well. Görli Testnet is a collective of individuals that believe that agnostic collaboration and coordination can be realistically achieved, and that tribalism can be dissolved and that community is what matters the most. Join our Riot.
We also need funds. We want to pay our contributors. If your community fund/entity/you as an individual, are willing to donate, we have a multi-sig. Thank you in advance!
